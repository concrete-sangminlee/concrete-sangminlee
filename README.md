# Sang Min Lee

Ph.D. candidate in Artificial Intelligence at Seoul National University working on machine learning for resilient infrastructure.

My work connects deep learning with engineering practice in wind engineering, structural health monitoring, and non-destructive testing. I care about methods that stay useful under real sensing constraints, remain reproducible in code, and are realistic to deploy outside benchmark settings.

## Research Themes
- AI for resilient infrastructure
- Structural wind engineering
- Structural health monitoring
- Non-destructive testing and evaluation
- Engineering-facing machine learning workflows
