# Sang Min Lee

Ph.D. candidate in Artificial Intelligence at **Seoul National University**, advised by **Prof. Thomas H.-K. Kang**.

I build machine learning systems for resilient infrastructure, with an emphasis on structural health monitoring, wind engineering, automated non-destructive testing, and field-ready AI for concrete structures.

My recent work spans impact-echo automation, lightweight crack-classification models, anomaly-aware monitoring, and retrieval-augmented assistants for structural design codes.

## Research Agenda
I focus on reliable, field-ready AI systems that connect sensing, diagnosis, and engineering decision support.

- Structural health monitoring
- Wind engineering and code-aware AI workflows
- Automated NDT for concrete inspection
- On-device models for engineering deployment
- LLM and RAG for structural design standards
